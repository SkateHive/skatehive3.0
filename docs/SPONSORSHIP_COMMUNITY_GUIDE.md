## Common Questions

### "What's a Lite Account?"

A lite account is someone who signed up with just email (or wallet/Farcaster). They can post immediately, but their posts don't earn Hive rewards yet. Think of it as a "trial account" that can be upgraded.

### "Why Do They Post via 'skateuser'?"

Technically, lite accounts don't have Hive blockchain accounts yet. So their posts are published by our shared account (`skateuser`), but we display their real name and avatar using an overlay system. Users don't see any difference - it looks like their own posts!

### "What Happens After Sponsorship?"

1. Real Hive account created (costs 3 HIVE)
2. Account keys generated and emailed to user
3. Posting key encrypted and stored securely
4. User continues logging in with email (no change!)
5. Future posts use their real Hive account
6. They earn rewards automatically

### "Do They Need to Manage Keys?"

**No!** That's the beauty of it:
- Keys are stored securely (encrypted)
- User logs in with email like always
- Posts automatically use their Hive account
- **Optional:** They can import keys to Keychain for advanced features

### "What If They Lose Their Keys?"

- Keys are emailed to them (saved in inbox)
- They can re-download from settings anytime
- Encrypted backup stored in our database
- They can always revoke and re-link via Keychain

## Troubleshooting

### "Sponsor button isn't working"

**Check:**
1. Is the user eligible? (5 posts, 7 days old, 3 voters)
2. Does the sponsor have enough HP? (50 minimum)
3. Has sponsor hit daily limit? (5 per day)
4. Is there a 24-hour cooldown active?

### "User didn't receive their keys email"

**Steps:**
1. Check spam folder
2. Re-send from settings page (they can log in)
3. Verify email address is correct
4. Contact support if still not received

### "Sponsored user's posts still showing as lite account"

**Possible causes:**
1. Account creation still processing (wait 5 minutes)
2. User needs to refresh their session (log out/in)
3. Cached profile data (clear cache)
4. Technical issue (escalate to dev team)
